 Instructions:
  1. Add these launch commands to Portal Prelude RTX properties: -nogamepadui -windowed
  2. Drag and drop one of the rtx.conf presets into Portal Prelude RTX directory, make sure to replace existing rtx.conf
  3. Set GPU clock speeds to 1600mhz and use Proton 9 for a smoother experience

 Recommended resolutions and fps limits for presets
  1280x800 or 1600x900

  Low: 60
  Medium: 40
  High: 30

  Warning: While in ALT + X dev menu, do not go to the remix graphics menu, this will revert some changes and reduce performance
